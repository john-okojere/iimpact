I-Impact Consult

I-Impact is a full-service law firm with a broad and extensive experience in corporate and commercial, company secretarial, regulatory compliance, litigation, intellectual property, project finance, media, real estate and telecommunications practices garnered over the numerous years of partnering and advising corporates, organizations and individuals. We also offer free bespoke counselling sessions on our Impact Mondays as well as other projects targeted at making significant impact in the lives of people.
We are duly registered to carry on legal practice in the Federal Republic of Nigeria and have a presence in Nigeria’s Federal Capital Territory, Abuja.

Legal Services

Banking And Finance Legal Service

We provide legal services on banking and finance matters to our clients including drafting and negotiating financial documentation such as loan agreements and syndicated loans, security instruments and other forms of guarantee. We advise and assist on specific transactions or matters relating to capital raising, securities offerings, capital restructuring, corporate and asset acquisitions and disposals, strategic investments and similar activities.

Litigation And Dispute Resolution

Our lawyers deploy the requisite tools and strategies to find creative and innovative solutions to the most challenging legal problems. Whether at trial or negotiating a settlement, our clients can rely on our impeccable advocacy in the courtroom and our skilful alternative dispute resolution techniques to maximize their chances of a desired result. We also help identify, assess and manage risks in relation to investment decisions and businesses to prevent possible loss.

Real Estate Services

We assist clients in sourcing their desired place of abode. Whether the search is for a house to rent or an outright purchase of land/houses, we have a network of agents and property managers who we work closely with to achieve the desired results.

Precious Chinazam Ijere

Precious is our founder and Managing Partner and a graduate of University of Uyo, Akwa-Ibom state.
